The environment over the chaos

Companion scheme for “A project never loses information. It loses its state.” One waterfall: people and agents keep working where they always did; on top of that stream the workspace — the environment itself — builds and maintains itself: topics are its units, the temporal model is its property, projections are how it is displayed.

①Contributors

Work happens where it always did — nothing is filed twice, no one moves anywhere.

PeopleThe team: messages, decisions, documents.
AgentsBots and AI agents: results of their work.
ServicesInformation systems, events and triggers.

②Sources

Connectors normalize events into one append-only stream of observations, with links back to the originals — interpretations never rewrite the past.

Messenger, mail, calendar, tracker, knowledge base, repository, analytics.

③Attach + Discovery

Deterministic pipeline: local, reproducible, versioned. The LLM sees only the residual doubt.

The gate Validation — a law of the environment

Derived structure earns its place through the environment's own rules: accuracy, completeness, consistency, usefulness, stability. The law is shared, the checks are tuned per source and contributor — different costs of error, different levels of trust.

Attach — known topicsStructural anchors first (thread, ticket, email chain, anchor post); semantics only as a floor; residual doubt → LLM or human.
Discovery — new workClusters of unattached observations → hypothesis topics. Identity survives recompute: insert · extend · merge · split · close · fade · reopen.

④Workspace — the environment

The part of the world that survives reasoning. Derived from observations, recomputable, self-maintaining — nobody curates a board.

The unit Topic — one line of work

What it is, what grounds its identity, what context it carries, what state it is in, where it is going. Death by default; rollback = one operation.

CoreHard identity anchors only. Defines the profile and attracts new observations.
HaloSemantic context. Visible to people and agents; never attracts.
The property Temporal model — state and trajectory

Sequence of states → movement vector, served with confidence. Activity is not progress: what happens now vs where it is going.

The view Projection layer — one world, many views

Each role — product manager, analyst, developer, lead, agent — reads the same facts through its own projection and attention tiers: focus acts and decides, periphery updates silently, background returns on a signal.
